# AllTeacher Extension: Open-Source Overview

AllTeacher is an open-source academic integrity platform designed to empower educators in the modern classroom. As artificial intelligence and digital tools become increasingly prevalent, AllTeacher provides instructors with a straightforward, reliable mechanism to monitor and discourage academic dishonesty without requiring advanced technical expertise or a background in computer science.

### Core Objectives
Empower Educators: Simplify digital classroom management by removing the technical barriers typically associated with monitoring software.

Promote Integrity: Discourage dishonest practices and misuse of artificial intelligence by fostering transparency in student workflows.

Track Authentic Progress: Provide actionable, real-time insights into student learning trajectories and assignment completion.

Key Capabilities
Process Tracking: Monitor and analyze student progress incrementally rather than assessing only the final submitted deliverable.

Intuitive Interface: Designed specifically for educators, ensuring seamless onboarding and operation without specialized training.

Transparent Analytics: Gain clear visual metrics regarding time spent, revision history, and engagement levels throughout an assessment.

### Why Open Source?
By open-sourcing the AllTeacher extension, we invite the global educational and developer communities to inspect, refine, and enhance the codebase. This collaborative approach ensures that the software remains secure, transparent, and continuously updated to meet the evolving challenges of digital learning environments.
